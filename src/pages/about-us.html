About us


Alkoshop is a place where you can definitely choose from a selection of quality alcohol. We are a team of enthusiasts for whom the search for the most interesting bottles from around the world is not only a job, but also a passion.

We bring you exclusively original and branded alcohol, which you can conveniently buy through our e-shop or in several brick-and-mortar stores in several corners of Slovakia. We strive to make the customer experience with us as pleasant as the enjoyment of sips of a quality distilled beverage.
