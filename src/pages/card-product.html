Absolut Vodka is a classic tasting vodka that is distilled from winter wheat. The recipe for its production is more than 30 years old, and the design of the bottle is based on a medicine bottle from the 18th century. It is clean and elegant, with touches of black peppercorns (a bit reminiscent of rye) and vanilla bean.
